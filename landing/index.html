Architecture

4 intelligence levels, 11 tools

LLMs understand optimization requests but cannot compute optimal solutions or quantify risk. OptimEngine does both.

L1 Deterministic

Optimal assignments under known constraints

Scheduling (FJSP), routing (CVRPTW), bin packing — each with 4 objectives.

optimize_schedule validate_schedule optimize_routing optimize_packing
L2 Uncertainty

What happens when parameters change?

Sensitivity maps, worst-case protection, Monte Carlo with VaR/CVaR risk metrics.

analyze_sensitivity optimize_robust optimize_stochastic
L2.5 Multi-obj

Trade-off frontiers for competing goals

Pareto frontier for 2-4 objectives. Correlation analysis and recommendations.

optimize_pareto
L3 Prescriptive

Forecast → Optimize → Risk → Recommend

Historical data in, actionable decisions out. 4 forecasting methods, 3 risk appetites.

prescriptive_advise
